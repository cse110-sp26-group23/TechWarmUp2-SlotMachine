### User Focus
- Primary:
  - graphics
    - win feedback (visual + audio)
  - free entry rewards

- Secondary:

### Takeaways
- Keep:
  - strong win animation + sound feedback
  - early free reward onboarding

- Improve:
  - perceived fairness / transparency of RNG
  - balance between rewards and spending pressure

- Avoid:
  - overly aggressive deposit prompting
  - feeling of forced loss cycles

- Add:
  - clearer reward probability / fairness signals
  - non-paying progression paths
