### Feature 4 — Collapsible Paytable
As a player on a small screen,
I want to hide the paytable when I'm not looking at it,
so that the reels and controls have more room to breathe.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
- Paytable collapses and expands via a toggle button
- The open/closed state is communicated to screen readers via aria-expanded
- Collapse and expand animate smoothly rather than jumping
