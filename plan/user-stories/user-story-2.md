### Feature 2 — Scrolling Reel Animation
As a player,
I want the reels to scroll and land on symbols smoothly,
so that each spin feels dynamic rather than symbols just snapping into place.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
- Each reel scrolls through random symbols before landing on the result
- Animation uses an easing curve that decelerates into the final position
- All reels stop in staggered order, left to right
