### Feature 6 — Win Visual Effects
As a player,
I want big and jackpot wins to trigger screen effects like confetti and flashes,
so that landing a major win feels like a real moment worth celebrating.
 
Acceptance Criteria:
- Small wins resolve with no screen effect
- Big wins trigger a screen flash overlay
- Jackpot wins spawn confetti particles with randomized colors, sizes, and durations
- Effects do not interfere with the reel layout or block the controls
